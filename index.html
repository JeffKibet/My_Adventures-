Adventures in fire Island

My trip to Fire Island was an unforgettable escape from the noise of everyday life a place where the moment you step off the ferry, the world slows down and nature takes over. The highlight of my adventure was hiking through the ancient Sunken Forest, where 200-year-old trees formed a breathtaking canopy overhead and white-tailed deer crossed our path without a care in the world. I paddled across the calm, glittering waters of the Great South Bay at sunrise, watched dolphins break the surface along the Atlantic shore, and ended each evening gathered around a crackling beach bonfire under the most star-filled sky I have ever seen. Fire Island has no roads, no cars, and no rush, just 32 miles of raw, beautiful coastline that reminds you what it feels like to truly be present. It was not just a trip. It was a reset.
